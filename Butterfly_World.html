BUTTERFLY WORLD

Butterflies are a diverse and colorful group of insects found throughout the world. With over 20,000 species, they come in a variety of sizes, shapes, and colors. Butterflies play an important role in pollination and are an indicator of the health of the ecosystem they inhabit. Many species are also popular with butterfly enthusiasts and can be found in butterfly gardens and conservatories. Some of the most well-known species include the Monarch butterfly, which migrates thousands of miles each year, and the Blue Morpho butterfly, with its striking iridescent blue wings.
